Ann Taylor is an American sportswear brand rooted in a heritage of work wear, iconic silhouettes, signature color, print and pattern that is considered Always Ann. The brand seeks to blend the best of sophisticated, fashionable design with notable quality and fit while staying one step ahead of the modern woman's needs and desires driven by her evolving lifestyle. Ann Taylor creates value for our customers through a unique combination of timeless, everyday-chic wardrobing that takes her to work and beyond.

It all began nearly 70 years ago with just a dream and a dress. Founder, Richard Liebeskind, dreamed of opening a shop. Then his dressmaker father gave him a gift. It was a shirt-dress called…the Ann Taylor. Nearly 70 years since the first Ann Taylor opened, the brands mission has never changed… help women look and feel confident and great. Through all the moments that matter.
