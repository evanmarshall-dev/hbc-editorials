How to choose a suit.

Shoulders.

A proper and naturally snug shoulder fit ensures the jacket is right.

Chest.

A jacket should button up easily with some room left inside for comfort and ease.

Length.

The hem should sit only an inch or two below your cuff.

Sleeves.

A cuff should line up to where the base of your thumb meets your wrist.

Trousers.

Tight or loose, the pant rise is your personal preference for style and comfort.
